TALKING WITH SPEECH IMPAIRED PERSON





This module provide the facility to communicate with hearing and speech impaired person. Now everybody do not need to learn sign language to talk with such people. This module reduces the communication gap with disable people. The sentences and words are converted to videos and images of sign language. In this way a person can convey message to such disable persons.
